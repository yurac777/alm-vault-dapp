2. Participate in a 50% B2B Revenue Share program via our institutional referral link.
3. Earn Epoch 1 $vALM Airdrop Points (3x Multiplier) to capture upside in the ALMVault governance token.

## 2. Value Proposition for the DAO
Holding idle USDC in the treasury exposes the DAO to opportunity costs. Traditional lending protocols (like Aave or Compound) currently yield 3-6% APY. ALMVault leverages these same battle-tested protocols (Aave V3) to borrow ETH and deploy it into high-fee Uniswap V3 pools, capturing trading fees while remaining delta-neutral.

**Key Benefits:**
- **Zero Lock-ups:** The vault uses ERC-4626. The DAO can withdraw its USDC at any time. 100% liquid.
- **Gas-Free Automation:** The ALM SRE Keeper rebalances positions automatically when prices move out of bounds. The DAO pays no gas fees for rebalancing.
- **Revenue Share:** If approved, ALMVault will provide the DAO with a custom B2B API endpoint and referral link. The DAO earns 50% of protocol fees generated by any retail users onboarded through the DAO's channels.

## 3. Risk Assessment & Math
Security of the DAO's capital is the highest priority. 

- **Smart Contract Risk:** ALMVault contracts are fully audited. We do not use proprietary AMMs; all funds reside in Aave V3 and Uniswap V3.
- **Impermanent Loss (IL) Mitigation:** The vault algorithmically borrows the exact amount of volatile asset (WETH) needed to pair with USDC. If ETH goes up, the Uniswap LP loses ETH but gains USDC. Simultaneously, the Aave debt increases in USD value. These two forces mathematically cancel out (Delta = ~0), leaving only the accrued trading fees as profit.
- **Liquidation Risk:** The SRE Keeper maintains a dynamic Health Factor on Aave (Base: 1.60, scaling up to 2.00 during high volatility). This provides a massive buffer against flash crashes, preventing liquidations.
